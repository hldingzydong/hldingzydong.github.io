#### [元音 + 元音](https://www.bilibili.com/video/av18738805?p=47)

| spell | prounciation |
| :-----: | :-----: |
| s**ee i**t | see(y)it |
| ver**y i**nteresting | ver(y)interesting |
| carr**y o**n | carr(y)on |

| ma**y I** | ma(y)i |
| enjo**y i**t | enjo(y)t |
| bo**y i**s | bo(y)is |


> 当第一个元音要求**嘴唇发音变圆**时，如/u/、/ou/,我们不把“u“音加进去，我们加入"w"音

## [轻读](https://www.bilibili.com/video/av18738805?p=48)
> 轻读功能词: 代词、介词、冠词和助动词(will am is do does),但这不意味着你可以说得很快,遇到实义词还是应当放慢语速

#### 轻读介词时，其中的元音一般变成了中性音/ə/

| spell | prounciation | example |
| :-----: | :-----: | :-----: |
| as | /əs/ | as long as you want |
| on | /ən/ | please come on time |
| at | /ət/ | I am at home |
| to | /tə/ | I'd like to talk to you |
| for | /fər/ | for a longtime | 
| or | /ər/ | yes or no (yes'r no) |
| our | /ər/ | This is our house.(is'r house) |
| and | /n/ | this and(/n/) that |

对于or,如果在思考或者不知道说什么,就延长发音，正常读.  
轻度不意味着语速很快,遇到实义词仍需放慢语速。 

- Sooner **or** later, you'll mee**t** **our** parents.  
- I went **to** work **at** nine **o**'clock.  

#### [轻读代词](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV15W411e7tF?p=49)
###### 以h为prefix  his him her 
> He did ... 中h发音(位于句首，或者强调时发音)  
> Did he？中h不发音，因此为Did e? 

###### 以th为prefix,同h

| type | spell | prounciation | sample |
| :-----: | :-----: | :-----: | :-----: |
| he | Could **h**e?<br>Should **h**e? | Coulde?<br>Shoulde? | He wants **h**er to know that **h**e loves **h**er. |
| him | I told **h**im<br>call **h**im | I toldm<br>callm | I‘ll talk to **h**im when I see **h**im. |
| her | love **h**er<br>drive **h**er | lover<br>driver | She cut **h**er hair and I didn't recognize **h**er. |
| them | pick **th**em up<br>need **th**em | pickem up<br>needem | I'll pick **th**em up and take **th**em home. |



## [缩写](https://www.bilibili.com/video/av18738805?p=50)
> 在American看来,口语中不使用缩写的人是初学者(**书面语中**或**强调某个意思时**不使用缩写)

- I'll: 在"I"和"l"中间加入轻声的/ə/
- it'll: 将"t"转为"d",it(d)'ll 

| spell | prounciation |
| :-----: | :-----: |
| could've(could have) | could/ə/ve |
| would've | would/ə/ve |
| would you have | would you/ə/ve |

| spell | prounciation |
| :-----: | :-----: |
| could not have(将d和n连读,ot消失) | couldn/ə/ve |
| would not have | wouldn/ə/ve |
